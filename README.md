## Featured Projects

### 1. Oil Well Profit Analysis
**Skills:** Linear Regression, Bootstrapping, Confidence Intervals, Risk Analysis, Business Decision-Making  
**Summary:** Built a model to predict oil reserves across three regions and used bootstrapping to estimate average profit, confidence intervals, and probability of loss in order to recommend the best drilling region.  
**Repository:** [oil-well-profit-analysis](https://github.com/roux542/oil-well-profit-analysis)

### 2. Taxi Demand Time Series Forecasting
**Skills:** Time Series Analysis, Feature Engineering, Random Forest, RMSE, TimeSeriesSplit  
**Summary:** Forecasted hourly taxi demand using lag features, rolling means, and machine learning models. Selected the best model with time-series cross-validation and achieved the required RMSE target.  
**Repository:** [taxi-demand-time-series](https://github.com/roux542/taxi-demand-time-series)

### 3. Video Game Sales Forecasting
**Skills:** Exploratory Data Analysis, Data Visualization, Hypothesis Testing, Business Insights  
**Summary:** Analyzed historical video game sales, user reviews, genres, and regional preferences to identify patterns of commercial success and support 2017 campaign planning.  
**Repository:** [video-game-sales-forecasting](https://github.com/roux542/video-game-sales-forecasting)
